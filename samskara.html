If you have been looking for a place to express your progressive Artistry, to partake in the initiation of political dialogue with the undying might of truth and engage in discussions that are oriented towards a better tomorrow, then you have reached the ideal destination of Art-Literature and Politics Combined at ‘Samskara GEC’! A platform to scrape and sharpen your creativity against in a proactively unbiased, brave and liberalist manner, aiming to rediscover the importance of Art in bringing out ideas that can revolutionise the World!
