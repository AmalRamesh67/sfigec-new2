Initiatives

Viva la Mensturation

In the wake of various religious organisations labelling menstruating women 'impure', Viva La Mensuration was an awareness campaign organised by Mathrukam spanning through a week upholding menstruation and menstrual health.

Zubaan

In a society where we are made to feel ashamed for the assaults that happen to us, where we are blamed because we were 'asking for it', Zubaan, an anonymous online platform, dispelled the hidden guilt and silence from thearts of every student. Recalling incidents from their childhood, their teenage and even their college years, many students spoke brutally and honestly about incidents that haunted them kickstarting a healing process.

No Go Zone

Sex Education is something that is visibly excluded from our education system. The repercussions of this are usually faced by women and other marginalised genders. No Go Zone' was an online campaign that dealt with sex education and the undiscussed topic of relationships and sex starting very necessary conversations in our campus.

Chembarathi

During its online phase, Mathrukam GEC organised 'Chembarathi', a nomination challenge aiming to raise awareness about menstrual cups and its benefits. Free menstrual cup distribution for students was also a part of the campaign.

SAAM

Mathrukam GEC observed SAAM (SEXUAL ASSUALT AWARENESS MONTH) during April. For students in a country where marital rape is still not penalized, bringing conversations about consent was of the utmost importance.

Pride Month

As a part of pride month celebrations, Mathrukam GEC conducted a Pride Month surveyed that revealed 30% of GECians identified themselves as LGBTQ+. A talk 'Queer Community and Kerala society' was also organized by Mathrukam on Clubhouse with five queer activists on the panel.
