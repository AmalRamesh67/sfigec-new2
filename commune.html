After all we engineers are meant to be working round the clock, so sitting idle is not really our cup of tea. With this in mind the initiative of GEC Commune was launched, in a bid to provide students with an opportunity to water their passion by working from home in various fields and earning a few bucks along the way too! A team of experts were summoned for each field during an initial training period, after which freelance opportunities were created for those who enrolled, in fields like graphic designing, content writing, web designing, digital marketing, architectural design and many many more.
